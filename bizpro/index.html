ABOUT FAWNOOS


Fawnoos is a term of Greek origin meaning 'light' or 'lantern'. It was historically used in its meaning of "the light of the world," and is a symbol of hope and guidance in Persian literature, as in "light in the darkness".

Support and consulting services for open-source technologies and platforms in the identity and access manegement (IAM) space. Focused on web single sign-on, multifactor authentication, federated identity, authentication protocols and integrations, and more.
